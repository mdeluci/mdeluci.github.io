Excited to announce that I was selected as a Rising Star in Computational and Data Sciences, an honor awarded jointly by Oden Institute for Computational Engineering and Sciences, Santa Fe Institute, and Los Alamos National Laboratory, Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, and Sandia National Laboratories.

The workshop at the Santa Fe Institute was a fantastic experience, from the stimulating discussions with a talented cohort of young scientists to the breathtaking scenery and great food. Thank you to the organizers, Karen Willcox Nicole Aretz Jim Stewart Aric Hagberg Aimee Hungerford Aleksandra Pachalieva, for creating such a memorable event.

Proud to represent Eli Lilly and Company and bring this recognition back to the work we do.
